ISTQB Foundation Level Certification (CTFL)

Validated Software Testing Expertise | Best Practices | Quality Assurance

This certification demonstrates my proficiency in the principles of software testing, quality assurance methodologies, and effective test design techniques. It validates my understanding of the software development lifecycle (SDLC) and my ability to contribute to high-quality software delivery.

Key Skills & Topics Covered
  • Software Testing Fundamentals

    Understanding the principles of software testing, including test levels, techniques, and the role of testing in the SDLC.

  • Test Design Techniques

    Applying effective test design techniques such as boundary value analysis, equivalence partitioning, and decision table testing.

  • Test Levels (Unit, Integration, System, Acceptance)

    Knowledge of different test levels and their importance in ensuring quality at each stage of the software development process.

  • Test Management & Reporting

    Expertise in managing tests, tracking defects, and producing test reports to ensure transparency and effectiveness in testing efforts.

  • Risk-Based Testing & Test Planning

    Focus on risk assessment to prioritize testing efforts and develop comprehensive test plans to address high-risk areas of the application.

I apply this knowledge in my role by developing and automating test scripts, identifying defects early through continuous testing, and ensuring software quality through rigorous test execution across different levels.
